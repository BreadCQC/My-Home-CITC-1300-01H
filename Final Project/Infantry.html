M46A3 Ravager

The ravager MBT is the standard mass produced tank of hexiron. Although small in size, they usually roll out in large numbers, bringing their total firepower to unimaginable levels. This makes them one of the most feared vehicles in any exoplanetary battlefield.

P55M8 Plasmyr Tank

Being a gigantic hover tank, it can traverse on water at high speeds. Equipped with a plasmyr cannon, the tank fires supercharged particles at superhigh velocities to inflict the highest penetration possible.
